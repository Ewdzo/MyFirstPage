• Anime: Haikyuu!!

    Haikyuu!! is one of my favorite animes of all time without a doubt. It's an amazing history about a volleyball team in the city of Miyagi how were once regional champions who had competed in nacional championships and with the entrance of 4 freshmans they are willing to come back. Haikyuu!! is available on Crunchyroll.

    Anime: Hunter x Hunter

    Hunter x Hunter is also one of the best I ever watched. The history revolves around Gon Freecs, a 12 year old, who were abandonned by his father who willingly left to become a Hunter. Gon wants to become a Hunter himself to better understand the motivation and ambition of his father. Hunter x Hunter is available on Crunchyroll.

    Anime: One Piece

    One Piece is my favorite anime at the time. The history is about Monkey D. Luffy, who aspires to be the King of The Pirates and find the long-lost One Piece. One Piece is available on Crunchyroll and on Netflix.

    Game: Skyrim

    Skyrim is one of my favorite games. The history goes about the resurgence of the ultimate Dragon Slayer, the Dragonborn, aswell as the abrupt return of dragons who were long-gone. Skyrim is available on Steam and can also be bought directly from Bethesda website.

    Game: Minecraft

    Minecraft is definetely the game I spent the most hours on. An open-world, survival-based game with total world managment available, allowing you to go from the most basic constructions to literal fuctional computers inside the game. Minecraft is available on Xbox Store and can also be bought directly from the Mojang website.
